# Magic Trick

UpSearch should not sound or behave like a bulk job application product.

The memorable idea is this:

> A student arrives with a technical point of view and a useful artifact before
> asking for an opportunity.

The system can research, structure, verify, and draft. A human must still make
the final non-obvious choice: which problem is genuinely interesting, what
original contribution is worth building, and what personal detail makes the
conversation real.

Do not optimize that judgment out of the product.
